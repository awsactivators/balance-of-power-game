How to Play

  1. Separate Chapter 1 and Chapter 2 cards (black background) from Chapter 3 cards (brown background) . DO NOT OPEN Chapter 3 cards yet.
  2. Divide the cards by clue fragment cards (yellow backgrounds) and chapter cards (blue border).
  3. Scan the QR codes on each card to listen to the sounds and dialogue clues.
  4. Piece together the audio clues and solve them by chapter.

To solve a chapter:

  • From the SOLVE CHAPTER page, enter the 4-digit code (bottom right of each card) of the chapter you wish to solve.
  • Then enter the 4-digit codes for each clue cards, in the order you that wish.
  • Once you solve Chapter 1 and Chapter 2, you can gain access to Chapter 3 cards.

To solve the full story:

  • Once all the chapters have been solved, you may enter the SOLVE STORY page.
  • Enter the 4-digit codes of each chapter in the order that you wish.
  • YOU NEED TO SOLVE ALL THE CHAPTERS before you can attempt solving the story.
